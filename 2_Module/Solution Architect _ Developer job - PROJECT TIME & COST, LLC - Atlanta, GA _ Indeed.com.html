Solution Architect / Developer

Project Time & Cost (PT&C) is seeking a Solution Architect / Developer to join our technology group (PT&C Digital). PT&C Digital develops enterprise-class, web-based software solutions that help clients manage their businesses with greater insight, efficiency and assurance.

The Solution Architect / Developer’s primary responsibility is to design, architect and develop new software products from concept to launch with involvement in all aspects of the software development lifecycle as part of our agile project team methodology.

The PT&C Digital group has the unique opportunity to operate like an entrepreneurial product company, building new, greenfield software products while being part of a larger organization with in-house expertise and a broad base of established clients.

This role provides an opportunity to be involved in the earliest stages of platform, product and solution development using the latest technologies, patterns and architectures.

ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Research, evaluate, select and implement new technologies, patterns and architecture efficiently and effectively
  • Architect and design optimal coding techniques that improve the quality of codebase
  • Architect and develop features across multiple components within our platform
  • Understand the business vision to optimize the business value of software solutions
  • Propensity to factor through complex problems to find the simplest solutions
  • Ability to abstract product designs to implement code solutions in the most generalized, componentized, comprehensive manner
  • Extreme attention to detail within an understanding of the big picture and vision
  • Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal with the capability of communicating to non-technical stakeholders reasoning for architectural decisions
  • Strong quantitative and analytical skills, including a strong sense of intellectual honesty
  • Document relevant architecture, development and configuration designs and information
  • Participate in defining plans for standardizing, scaling and enhancing current processes and configurations (dev processes, continuous integration, deploy)
  • Support software engineers by providing advice, coaching and mentoring

DESIRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE:

  • Experience as a software architect /developer on greenfield projects in a small team or company environment
  • Experience architecting and developing multi-tier, scalable, globally dispersed enterprise applications, running in a highly distributed manner
  • Experience with the latest technologies and patterns relevant for platform based systems
  • Demonstrated record of successfully delivering complex projects with demanding deadlines
  • Experience providing technical leadership, guidance and mentorship

TECHNOLOGIES

  • Angular 2/4
  • Command/Query pattern
  • JavaScript
  • CSS

  • WebAPI
  • C# (asynchronous with async/await)
  • Entity Framework

  • Domain Driven Design
  • Micro service Architecture
  • Microsoft Azure Development Platform

About PT&C Digital

Founded in 1982, Project Time & Cost, LLC (PT&C) is a global leader in project and program management, construction management, cost and schedule management, earned value management, and technology solution services. PT&C provides independent, expert oversight and analysis to ensure control of scope, cost, schedule, risk, and quality.

PT&C’s growing digital division (PT&C Digital) is focused on delivering technology-enabled solutions and products to our global list of clients. Our vision is to further expand the company’s reach into both private and public markets by combining technology with our established industry expertise to provide holistic, comprehensive solutions to our clients.

Successes in program management and construction cost estimating and management have earned PT&C a respected reputation and numerous acknowledgments including:

  • Engineering News-Record Top 50 Program Management Firms (2011-2016)
  • Engineering News-Record Top 100 Construction Management-for-Fee Firms (2011-2016)
  • Winner of U.K. NDA Estate Supply Chain Award for Best Example of Supplier ‘Going that Extra Mile’ (2012)
  • Recognized as Patriotic Employer by the Office of the Secretary of Defense and Employer Support for the Guard and Reserve for supporting employees’ military service (2012)
